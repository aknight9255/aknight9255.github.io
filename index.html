About Me

Previous Next

I joined the workforce as a senior in high school and as most do, started in the fast food industry. Over the course of the following years I would enter into various customer service rolls. Eventually I found myself in the role of a Real-Time Analyst at RCI in 2016. This was my first introduction to a world outside of customer service. In this role I was manipulating and investigating data with a team. Everyday was a new challenge and there was always something new to learn.

At the end of 2018 my department faced cutbacks and I found myself looking at a fork in the road. Was I to enter back into the world of customer service or explore the word of data that I had started to fall in love with? After hours of research and more than a few pep talks I enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy. The world of coding is an ocean, and I have definitely thrown myself into it with both feet. If you asked me a year ago if I would be barreling down the path to becoming a .NET developer I might have laughed. Life is funny that way.

Now that you know a little about me, please explore the page more! You will find projects I have worked on, an easy to use contact box, and links to other platforms for additional information.
